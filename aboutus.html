About Us

Welcome to ECOWATT – Empowering E-Waste Recycling for a Sustainable Future!

At ECOWATT, we believe that managing electronic waste is not just a responsibility, but an opportunity to make a lasting difference. Our platform is dedicated to simplifying e-waste recycling, helping individuals and businesses dispose of their devices responsibly and securely, all while contributing to a greener planet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce e-waste and promote sustainability by making recycling as accessible, safe, and rewarding as possible. From pick-up scheduling to secure data deletion, we provide the tools and resources needed to encourage environmentally conscious choices in technology disposal.

What We Do

  • Schedule Convenient Pickups: Easily arrange pickups for unwanted devices without hassle.
  • Locate Nearby Drop-Off Points: Find the nearest certified recycling centers to dispose of electronics safely.
  • Data Security: Use our secure data-erasing tools to protect your personal information before recycling.
  • Earn Rewards: Get rewarded for every item recycled, with incentives that foster sustainable practices.

Our Impact

In a world where millions of devices are discarded every year, ECOWATT is on a mission to reduce the environmental impact of e-waste. By partnering with certified recyclers and eco-conscious brands, we ensure that your old electronics are processed responsibly, reducing pollution and conserving resources. Together, we’re building a cleaner, more sustainable future.

Join Us on Our Journey

We invite you to join the ECOWATT community as we work towards a world where technology and sustainability go hand in hand. Whether you're a consumer, a business, or a recycler, ECOWATT is here to support your eco-friendly journey, one device at a time.

Because Every Small Step Counts.
